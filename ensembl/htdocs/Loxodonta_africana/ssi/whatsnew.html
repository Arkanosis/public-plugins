What's New in Ensembl 41

Loxodonta africana News

  • RNA updates

    Ensembl RNA data has been updated as follows:

    • New ncRNAs for Zebrafish, Chimp, Medaka, Elephant, Rabbit, Armadillo and Tenrec
    • Updated miRNAs for all chordates

General News
