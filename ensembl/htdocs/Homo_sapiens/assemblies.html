Assemblies in NCBI Build 36

The following labels are used in Ensembl FASTA files etc to identify the various assemblies included in build NCBI 36:

reference
Human Genome Sequencing Consortium finished genome, release 4.
Celera
Celera Genomics whole genome shotgun assembly (AADB), November 2001. This assembly included both WGS and BAC sequence data.
CRA_TCAGchr7v2
The Center for Applied Genomics at the Hospital for Sick Children (Toronto) chromosome 7 assembly, (April 2004).
DR53
DR53 haplotype for the MHC region on chromosome 6.
c5_H2
An alternative assembly for part of chromosome 5.
c6_COX
An alternative haplotype assembly of the chromosome 6 MHC region based on sequence data from the COX library.
c6_QBL
An alternative haplotype assembly of the chromosome 6 MHC region based on sequence data from the QBL library.
c22_H2
An alternative assembly for part of chromosome 22.
