What's New in Ensembl 45

Homo sapiens News

  • New functional genomics data
    As of release 45, Ensembl Human will include a "Regulatory Build", integrating 8 genome-wide datasets to provide a single "best guess" set of regulatory elements.
    Read more...
  • CAGE data
    New human CAGE ditags and updated mouse CAGE ditags have been added to Ensembl 45.
  • CCDS update
    Some transcripts that were dropped from the CCDS set owing to poor quality have now been removed from Ensembl, and the stable ID archive updated accordingly.
  • Overlapping assembly entries
    Some overlapping entries in the mappings between NCBI35 and NCBI36 (on human) and NCBIm35 and NCBIm36 (on mouse) have been removed from Ensembl 45.
