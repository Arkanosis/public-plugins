What's New in Ensembl 47

Homo sapiens News

  • New genebuild on human assembly NCBI 36
    A new Ensembl genebuild has been run on the existing Homo sapiens assembly, NCBI 36. This has allowed us to incorporate new evidence and improve the number and accuracy of gene predictions.
  • Functional Genomics

    Ensembl 47 sees a new version of the regulatory build for Human, including extensive CD4 data.

  • Variation updates
    • New human database
      • Added SNPs/genotype data from Craig Venter and Jim Watson genomes, and merged Craig Venter's data into Celera reads HuBB.

General News
