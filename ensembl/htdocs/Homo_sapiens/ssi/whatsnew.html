What's New in Ensembl 36

  • Variation databases
    New SNPs from dbSNP_125 have been added to the variation databases for HUman, Mouse, Rat, Dog and Chicken.
  • CisRed regulatory features

    The CisRed regulatory features have been reloaded (naming format and factor associations changed). The "search regions" have also been imported, and can now be displayed as an extra track in ContigView.

  • Monthly cDNA update
    The human cDNA database has been updated as usual.
