  • Small discrepancy between Ensembl and Mart
    Please note that, owing to a small data bug which was patched in homo_sapiens_core_41_36c after the Mart database had been produced, 21 miRNA genes and 92 miRNA transcripts have different stable IDs in the core database and the Mart database.
