What's New in Ensembl 42

Gallus gallus News

  • New Chicken assembly and genebuild
    Ensembl Gallus gallus has been updated to the May 2006 assembly, and features a new genebuild utilising chicken and other vertebrate proteins.
  • Variation update on new assemblies
    Ensembl Dog and Chicken have dbSNP entries updated to map onto the new assembly and genebuild.
