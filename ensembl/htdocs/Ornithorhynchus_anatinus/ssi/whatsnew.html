Ornithorhynchus anatinus News

  • Variation updates

    Platypus transcript_variation re-run to reflect the changed gene set

  • Platypus patches and updates
    Some genes have been removed from Ensembl Platypus as they have been identified by the Oxford group as being incorrect or contaminants. We have also re-run the xrefs, affy mappings and stable ID mapping.
