  • New species - Rhesus macaque
    Ensembl is pleased to announce completion of the genebuild on the Rhesus macaque, Macaca mulatta, based on the preliminary (0.1) assembly from the Baylor College of Medicine Human Genome Sequencing Center.
  • New Cow assembly and genebuild
    Ensembl Cow now includes a genebuild on the latest Bos taurus 6x assembly. The improved assembly has allowed the use of a 'classical' gene-build to determine the gene-set.
  • New Fugu assembly and genebuild
    Ensembl now contains an import of the latest assembly and genebuild of Takifugu rubripes from the Fugu Genome Project in Singapore.
