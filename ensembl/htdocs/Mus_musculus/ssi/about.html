About the Mouse genome

Assembly

Ensembl Mouse is based on the NCBI m35 mouse assembly (December 2005, strain C57BL/6J).

The Mouse Genome Sequencing Consortium is a joint project between The Whitehead Institute/MIT Center for Genome Research, The Washington University Genome Sequencing Center, The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute and EMBL - EBI to provide the Mouse genome sequence to the world. We work closely with other Mouse groups to provide an integrated resource (see below for credits).

Annotation

This site provides a full Ensembl gene build for the above assembly. There are some major changes in the assembly; for more details see the NCBI build statistics .

Modifications to the systems have further improved the gene set. 95% of the known genes and 68% of the novel genes from build m34 retain the same Ensembl gene ids in this release. There will also be a new build on m36 soon.
