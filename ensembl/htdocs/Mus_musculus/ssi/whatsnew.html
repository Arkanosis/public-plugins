What's New in Ensembl 42

Mus musculus News

  • Updates to Mouse databases

    Ensembl Mus musculus has updated cDNAs, and the following additions made to the core database:

    • Patched gene set, including Havana transcripts imported from the CCDS set
    • New PET ditag mappings
  • Variation updates
    Ensembl Human and Mouse variation databases have been updated to match the patched gene set
