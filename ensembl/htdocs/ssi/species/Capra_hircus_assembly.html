The ARS1 assembly was submitted by USDA ARS on 24/08/2016. The assembly is on the Chromosome level, consisting of 30,399 contigs assembled into 29,907 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 26,244,591 while the scaffold N50 is 87,277,232 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_001704415.1
