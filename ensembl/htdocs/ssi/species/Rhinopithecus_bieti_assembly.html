The ASM169854v1 assembly was submitted by the Laboratory for Conservation and Utilization of Bio-resource at Yunnan University on 2016/08/03. The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 226,509 assembled into 105,032 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 36,417 while the scaffold N50 is 2,225,337.
