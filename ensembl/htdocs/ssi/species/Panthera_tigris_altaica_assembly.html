The PanTig1.0 assembly was submitted by Personal Genomics Institute on 2013/09/06 . The assembly is on the Scaffold level, consisting of 157,032 assembled into 1,479 scaffolds. The N50 size is the length such that 50% of the assembled genome lies in blocks of the N50 size or longer. The N50 length for the contigs is 30,032 while the scaffold N50 is 8,860,407 The genome assembly represented here corresponds to GenBank Assembly ID: GCA_000464555.1
