About Ensembl

Ensembl creates, integrates and distributes reference datasets and analysis tools that support research in comparative genomics, evolution, sequence variation and transcriptional regulation..

We annotate genes, compute multiple alignments, predict regulatory function and collect disease data. Ensembl tools include BLAST, BLAT, BioMart and the Variant Effect Predictor (VEP) for all supported species.
