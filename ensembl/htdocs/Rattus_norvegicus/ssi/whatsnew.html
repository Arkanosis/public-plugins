What's New in Ensembl 42

Rattus norvegicus News

  • Rat database patched
    Ensembl Rattus norvegicus core database has been patched to remove several contigs (and their associated features) which were not part of the assembly, and which caused problems in the previous release.
  • Rat variation
    Ensembl Rat now includes dbSNP125 and SNPs from Celera reads (both SNPs and SARA SNPs, where the allele is the same as the reference one).
