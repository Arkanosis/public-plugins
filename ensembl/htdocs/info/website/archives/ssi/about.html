About Archive Ensembl

The main Ensembl site (www.ensembl.org) and the mirror sites are updated with the latest data on a monthly basis.

The Ensembl Archive sites have been set up so there is a stable link for three years to pages from a particular release (e.g. version 75 from February 2014) in Ensembl.

These links will be suitable for referring to Ensembl pages in publications.

A few older archives may be maintained if they include significant datasets for key species, but the availability of these sites cannot be guaranteed.

As from August 2014 we are supporting the human GRCh37 assembly at our dedicated GRCh37 human site. Unlike the usual Ensembl archive sites, this will be maintained and updated with new features with each release of Ensembl. Annotation on the human NCBI36 assembly is available at our Ensembl 54 archive site.

For all enquiries, please contact the Ensembl HelpDesk.
