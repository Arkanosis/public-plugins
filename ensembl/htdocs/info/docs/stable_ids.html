Ensembl stable IDs

Ensembl annotation uses a system of stable IDs that have prefixes based on the species scientific name plus the feature type, followed by a series of digits, e.g. ENSG00000139618. The prefixes in current use are:

Feature prefixes

G gene
T transcript
E exon
P protein

Species prefixes
