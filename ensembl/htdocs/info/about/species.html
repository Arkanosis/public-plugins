Other Metazoa

Plants and Fungi

Ensembl currently includes Brewer's Yeast, Saccharomyces cerevisiae, for comparative purposes.

A wider range of plant and fungal genomes will soon be available from EnsemblPlants and EnsemblFungi

Protists, Bacteria and Archaea

Unicellular and non-eukaryotic genomes can be found at EnsemblProtists and EnsemblBacteria
