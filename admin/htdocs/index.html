Static Content

The static documentation for Ensembl is maintained by a number of teams spread across the Sanger Institute and the EBI. To help ensure that these pages are a) consistent in appearance, b) easy to navigate and c) play nicely with the rest of the webcode, it is essential for contributors to follow our guidelines for content creation.

  • Files and links - where to put your files
  • Navigation - how to code your HTML headers to get your files included in (or excluded from) the automated navigation
  • XHTML Quick Reference - tips for writing documents that won't break Ensembl :)
